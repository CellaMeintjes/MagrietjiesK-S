About Us

Magrietjie’s Knots and Stitches isn't just a small business – it's a family legacy, connecting generations, and celebrating craftsmanship and creativity. It all began with a granddaughter and her skilled grandmother who shared a deep passion for sewing, knitting, and crocheting. The grandmother's patient teachings instilled not only the techniques but also a profound love for textiles and crafts.

The special bond between them flourished as they embraced new creative projects, often met with an enthusiastic "YES!" from the grandmother. Their local fabric and wool shop became their haven as they carefully selected the finest materials. This bond ultimately gave rise to Magrietjie’s Knots and Stitches, with the duo crafting beautiful clothing and accessories, driven by a shared dedication to quality and creativity.

Their handmade creations garnered widespread appreciation and led to the inception of this small business. It was born from a desire to share their rich tradition and craftsmanship with the world. At its core, Magrietjie’s Knots and Stitches stands for quality, creativity, and community. The brand is committed to preserving family traditions and invites you to be a part of their story by bringing their lovingly crafted products into your life. Thank you for joining them on this journey.
